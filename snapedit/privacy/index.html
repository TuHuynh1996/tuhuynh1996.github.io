Privacy Policy

SnapEdit — Last updated: April 10, 2026

We collect zero data

Summary

SnapEdit does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. All image editing happens entirely on your device.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No images, file names, or metadata are transmitted
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports are sent automatically

Network Usage

SnapEdit makes no network requests except for optional In-App Purchase transactions handled securely by Apple's StoreKit. No images or user data are ever uploaded.

File Access

The app accesses only the images you explicitly paste from the clipboard, open via the file picker, or drag and drop. Exported files are saved to the location you choose. No files are copied, cached, or stored in any hidden location.

In-App Purchases

SnapEdit offers an optional one-time donation via Apple's In-App Purchase system. This transaction is handled entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

SnapEdit does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Apple App Store

If you download SnapEdit through the Mac App Store, Apple may collect usage data according to their own privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
