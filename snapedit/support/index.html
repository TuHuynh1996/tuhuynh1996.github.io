Keyboard Shortcuts

  • Ctrl+Shift+V — Paste from anywhere (global hotkey)
  • Cmd+V — Paste / Overlay paste
  • Cmd+O — Open image file
  • Cmd+N — New blank canvas
  • Cmd+C — Copy to clipboard
  • Cmd+S — Save as PNG/JPEG
  • Cmd+K — Crop
  • Cmd+Z — Undo
  • Cmd+Shift+Z — Redo

Tool Shortcuts

  • B — Pen (Brush)
  • H — Highlighter
  • E — Eraser
  • R — Rectangle
  • O — Circle (Oval)
  • A — Arrow
  • L — Line
  • T — Text
  • N — Numbered annotation
  • X — Privacy blur

FAQ

Q: Is SnapEdit free?

Yes! SnapEdit is completely free with no limits. There's an optional $2.99 donation to support development.

Q: Does SnapEdit upload my images?

No. All editing happens locally on your Mac. No images are ever uploaded or transmitted.

Q: How do I restore my purchase?

Open About > Support > Restore Purchase.

Q: What macOS version is required?

macOS 14.0 (Sonoma) or later.
