Privacy Policy

PDF SecureConvert — Last updated: March 24, 2026

We collect zero data

Summary

PDF SecureConvert does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device with no internet connection required.

Data Collection

We do not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No file names, file contents, or metadata are transmitted
  • No cookies, tracking pixels, or advertising identifiers
  • No crash reports are sent automatically

Network Usage

PDF SecureConvert makes zero network requests. The app does not connect to any server, API, or external service. All file processing happens locally on your device.

File Access

The app accesses only the files and folders you explicitly select through the file picker or drag and drop. Processed files are saved to the location you choose. No files are copied, cached, or stored in any hidden location.

Third-Party Services

PDF SecureConvert does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Apple App Store

If you purchase PDF SecureConvert through the Mac App Store, Apple may collect purchase and usage data according to their own privacy policy. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app does not require any personal information to function.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, the changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since the app collects no data, we do not anticipate significant changes.
